## Data Retention and Deletion

We implement comprehensive data retention policies to comply with privacy regulations and minimize data storage. For detailed technical implementation, see [/docs/SECURITY.md](/docs/SECURITY.md).

| Data Category | Retention Period | Justification |
|--------------|------------------|---------------|

We run automated data purge jobs:

**Immediate Actions:**
1. Your personal information is anonymized
2. Active resumes and cover letters are soft-deleted
3. Exported files are permanently removed
4. Authentication credentials are invalidated

**Preserved Data (Anonymized):**
- Payment records (7 years - legal requirement)
- Audit logs (anonymized user references)
- Aggregated analytics (no personally identifiable information)

For more details on our deletion process, see [User Deletion Path](/docs/SECURITY.md#user-deletion-path).

## Your Privacy Rights

### Under GDPR (European Users)

You have the following rights:

**1. Right of Access (Article 15)**
- Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
- Receive information about how we process your data

**2. Right to Rectification (Article 16)**
- Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data

**3. Right to Erasure / "Right to be Forgotten" (Article 17)**
- Request deletion of your personal data
- Exceptions: legal obligations (e.g., tax records)

**4. Right to Restriction of Processing (Article 18)**
- Limit how we use your data in certain circumstances

**5. Right to Data Portability (Article 20)**
- Receive your data in machine-readable format (JSON)
- Transfer your data to another service

**6. Right to Object (Article 21)**
- Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- Opt-out of marketing communications

**7. Rights Related to Automated Decision-Making (Article 22)**
- Our AI suggestions are advisory only; final decisions are yours
- You can disable AI features in account settings

### Under CCPA (California Users)

California residents have additional rights:

**1. Right to Know**
- Categories of personal information collected
- Sources of personal information
- Business purpose for collection
- Third parties with whom we share data

**2. Right to Delete**
- Request deletion of personal information
- Exceptions: legal obligations and legitimate business needs

**3. Right to Opt-Out**
- We do NOT sell personal information
- You can opt-out of data sharing for marketing purposes

**4. Right to Non-Discrimination**
- We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights

### Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights:

1. **Account Settings**: Many actions can be done directly in your account
2. **Email Request**: Contact privacy@sexyresume.com
3. **Data Export**: Use the "Download My Data" feature in account settings
4. **Account Deletion**: Use the "Delete Account" option in account settings

**Response Time**: We will respond to requests within:
- GDPR: 30 days (may extend to 60 days for complex requests)
- CCPA: 45 days (may extend to 90 days with notice)

**Identity Verification**: We may request additional information to verify your identity before processing requests.
